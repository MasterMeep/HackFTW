Our Goal

Our goal is to connect users like you to different small charities globally. By doing this, we aim to help these charities grow, and "reach the stars".

What our tool does

This tool helps small charities find donators and gain the funding required to get closer to their goals in 3 simple steps

Choose

Within the region you have chosen, select a charity you would like to donate to.

Pinpoint

Open our provided map and select a region with charities you would like to support, along with a cause.

Connect

Connect to the charity of your choice, and donate to them directly
